sep 18, 2016
It was not a dream she could feel the warmth of the sun and the kiss of a gentle breeze she was in a world, a world whose inhabitants stared right past her without the least knowledge of her existence she was not a phantom and was as alive as anyone could be yet she was invisible she drifted to places unknown to mankind though she never moved an inch the people there became her new family and mixed emotions lingered in the air at good times she smiled with pleasure and cried her heart out when there was a misfortune but her laughter and tears were never heard she was alone invisible yet happy she could go on for days without food or sleep but alas when she came to the end of the world the world created by authors tears rolled down her eyes and she finally turned the last page of the book with a heavy heart.
